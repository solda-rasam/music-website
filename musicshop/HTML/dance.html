dance

It is a type of music that is created to accompany and assist dancing. This music can be a complete piece of music or part of a longer arrangement. In terms of performance, it is divided into two categories: live dance music and recorded dance music . Dance can be considered the initiator of new music styles such as disco, electronic dance and dance pop, and as its name suggests, its goal is to create happy music that is suitable for dancing
