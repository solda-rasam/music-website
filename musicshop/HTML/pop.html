pop

Pop music is usually contrasted with and distinguished from classical, folk, and techno music, and its greater popularity with women and female voices than rock music is one of its main characteristics. In many countries with reliable rankings, the majority, up to two-thirds, of the works in this genre have been by female singers. Artists in this genre can also work in the genres of trance, house, mash-ups, remixes, electronic music, rock, hip-hop, dance, rhythm and blues (R&B), and country, making it a flexible genre. Many branches such as trance, house, hard trance, and so on originate from the pop genre. The term "pop music" can also refer to a specific subgenre (within pop), such as soft rock and pop rock.
