classic

It is a general term used to refer to European art music. The beginning of this music is considered to be from the Middle Ages around 500 AD. It is worth noting that classical music and the classical period are two separate categories, the first refers to the style and the second to the historical period. Fernando Sor, Mateo Carcasi, Francisco Targa and Andrés Segovia are considered among the greatest classical guitar players. For a prominent guitar player, the choice of guitar strings is very important and essential.
