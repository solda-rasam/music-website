deephouse

شIt is a subgenre of house music that emerged in the 1980s by blending elements of Chicago house and jazz-funk with elements of soul music. Deep house music typically has a tempo of 120–130 beats per minute (BPM), contrasting with the slower tempo of hip hop (100 BPM) and the faster tempo of electronic-techno rave music (150–180 BPM). Deep house music has an acoustic feel. The online music store Beatport is credited with popularizing the genre, which gradually gave rise to future house.
