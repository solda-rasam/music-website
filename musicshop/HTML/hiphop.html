hip hop

Hip hop music typically consists of one or more rappers telling autobiographical stories, often about an imaginary counterpart, in highly rhythmic lyrics filled with techniques such as vowel assonance, phonetic alliteration, and rhyme. The rapper is accompanied by a composition, often referred to as a "beat", played by a DJ, and created by a producer, or one or more musicians. Beats are often composed using a combination of percussion from another song, usually a funk or soul song. In addition to the drums, other sounds are often synthesized, electronic, or performed. Sometimes a section can be an instrument, a sign of the DJ's or producer's skill.
