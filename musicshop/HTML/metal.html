metal

It is a subgenre of rock music that emerged as a defined musical style in the 1970s. It has its roots in the music of British and American hard rock bands who, inspired by blues rock and psychedelic rock and a mixture of massive, harsh (distorted) and amplified guitar sounds, long electric guitar solos and heavy beats, created "heavy metal music". The instruments used in this style are mainly electric guitar, bass guitar and drums, which are added to this collection in various branches of other instruments such as keyboards, violin, cello and acoustic guitar.
