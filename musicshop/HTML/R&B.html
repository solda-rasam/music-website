R&B

It is a style of Western music that is derived from rhythm and blues. This style, which was formed in the 1990s, is a combination of soul, funk, and hip-hop music. Famous and successful people in this field include Michael Jackson, Stevie Wonder, Whitney Houston, Mariah Carey, Adele, Beyonce, and Amir Tatalou.
