jazz

A musical style of the late 19th and early 20th centuries characterized by the use of sad notes, syncopation and counter-beats, long-short and long beats (swing), wide dynamic changes, the use of call and response, polyrhythmism and, most importantly, improvisation; and it is considered the first art form to emerge in the United States . A musical group that plays jazz is called a jazz band As jazz spread around the world, this music had a great influence on the musical cultures of different nations, regions, and places, as well as the growth of many other significant styles. New Orleans jazz, which emerged in the early 1910s, is a combination of traveling brass bands, ragtime, blues music with abundant improvisation
