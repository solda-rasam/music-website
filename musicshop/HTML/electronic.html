Electronic

Electronic music is a type of music that uses electronic instruments and electronic music technology in its production stages, or does not use acoustic instruments in the production of sounds. In general, its main difference from other types of music is the type of sound produced by electronic instruments. Examples of electromechanical sound production devices include the teleharmonium, the Hammond organ, and the electric guitar. Theremins, synthesizers, and computers can also be used to produce purely electronic sounds. Electronic music is generally non-verbal.
